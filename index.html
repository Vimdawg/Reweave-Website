From Their Hands to Their Future. Play It Forward

Transforming discarded denim into beautiful, sustainable pickleball cases while empowering local artisans

Carry Your Paddle. Carry a Purpose

We turn discarded denim into durable, batik-accented pickleball paddle cases. Each one is hand-crafted by B40 artisans—reducing textile waste while creating dignified, paid work

The Problem

In Malaysia, poverty isn’t just too little money, it’s too few chances: over 161,000 single mothers, most earning under RM2,000 a month, face 70% with no savings and 45% without childcare that limits work, while joblessness drives a 17% recidivism rate, closing the door for women to rebuild and for children to learn and play.

Our Solution

We craft recycled-material pickleball paddle cases, designed to carry your paddle and essentials, adorned with batik, providing paid tailoring work for single and formerly incarcerated moms, while reinvesting percentages of every sale into partner programs that support their re-entry and children’s access to sport

Our Journey & Roadmap

This is just the beginning. We have a clear, actionable 10-week plan to move from concept to a fully operational social enterprise, hitting key milestones for production, sales, and impact measurement along the way.

Phase 1: Validation (Weeks 1-3)

Finalized prototype based on user feedback and successfully completed Midpoint Pitch.

Phase 2: Proof of Concept (Weeks 4-6)

Onboard artisans, begin first production run, launch pre-orders, and host first pop-up booth.

Phase 3: Scaling (Weeks 7-10)

Launch on online marketplaces, measure and report social impact metrics, and deliver Final Pitch.
